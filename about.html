Who we are_

Breif Description

We are a club at Hamilton High School established in 2016 and dedicated to computer science and fun. Our meetings take place every Friday, during lunch in room 705 or in room 405.


The Contributors

Our Co-Founders are Jeshwanth Bheemanpally and Tommy Seo. We hope that many students from all backgrounds of life will take on the role of representing our club. Positions include: President, Vice President, Treasurer, Secretary


Our Mission Statement

We stand to spread the knowledge and fun of computer science to everyone, and discover new technology with teamwork and creativity.
